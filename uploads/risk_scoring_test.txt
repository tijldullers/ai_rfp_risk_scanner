AI System RFP Test Document

This RFP outlines requirements for an AI-powered customer service chatbot. The system must:

1. Process personal customer data including names, email addresses, and service history
2. Implement GDPR-compliant data processing and storage
3. Provide explainable AI decision making
4. Ensure robust cybersecurity measures
5. Include bias detection and mitigation capabilities
6. Comply with EU AI Act requirements for high-risk AI systems
7. Implement proper data governance and accountability measures
8. Ensure operational reliability and technical performance standards

The system will handle sensitive customer information and make automated decisions that could significantly impact customer experience and business operations.
