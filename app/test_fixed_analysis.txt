
AI Risk Assessment Test Document

This RFP requests the implementation of an AI-powered customer service chatbot system that will handle customer inquiries, process personal data, and make automated decisions regarding customer support escalation.

Key Requirements:
- Process customer personal information including names, email addresses, and support history
- Implement machine learning algorithms for sentiment analysis and intent recognition  
- Provide automated responses to common customer queries
- Integrate with existing CRM systems and databases
- Ensure GDPR compliance for EU customers
- Implement cybersecurity measures to protect customer data
- Establish governance framework for AI decision-making
- Meet regulatory compliance requirements including EU AI Act
- Manage operational risks during system deployment
- Address financial and commercial considerations
- Ensure ethical AI practices and bias mitigation
- Plan comprehensive implementation and change management strategy

This test document should trigger comprehensive analysis across all risk categories.
