AI Chatbot RFP for Customer Service

We are seeking an AI chatbot solution to handle customer inquiries. The system should:

1. Process customer personal data including names, emails, and transaction history
2. Use machine learning algorithms to provide personalized responses
3. Integrate with our existing CRM system
4. Handle sensitive financial information
5. Provide automated decision-making for loan applications
6. Store conversation data for training purposes
7. Use third-party AI models for natural language processing

Key Requirements:
- GDPR compliance for EU customers
- Data encryption and security measures
- Audit trails for all AI decisions
- Bias detection and mitigation
- Integration with external APIs
- Real-time processing capabilities

The solution must comply with all applicable data protection regulations and AI governance frameworks.
