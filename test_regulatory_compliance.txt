
Test RFP for AI Chatbot Implementation

We are seeking proposals for implementing an AI-powered customer service chatbot that will handle customer inquiries, process personal data including names and email addresses, and integrate with our existing CRM system.

The AI system will need to:
- Process customer personal data
- Learn from customer interactions  
- Provide automated responses
- Store conversation logs
- Integrate with external APIs

We require compliance with GDPR, cybersecurity best practices, and AI ethics guidelines.
